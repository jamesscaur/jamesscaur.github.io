Welcome!

Do whatever you want on here.

This webpage is fully editable. Try it out! You can edit whatever you want, and it works just like a normal writing program.

The box below (the "styleBox") is for advanced users. You can tweak the style and design of the webpage.

    e.g:
  • try changing "color: black;" to "color: red;" in the "#text" section in the styleBox.
  • try changing "background: white;" to "background: pink;" in the ".everything" section of the styleBox.
  • try changing "font-family:Helvetica Neue;" to "font-family:Impact" in the "#text" section of the styleBox.

You can also save your changes. It only saves to your web browser at the moment, but I'm working on a solution that will let you share your webpage with others.

Thanks for checking this out!

    Tips:
  • You can write colors in a lot of ways. You can use words (e.g blue, red, green, silver), but you can also use the computer code for a color (e.g #ccc)
  • Here is a simple color picker: |
  • Click the [x] on the styleBox when you're done to make it go away. (warning: there's no going back)
  • Click 'delete save' to reset your webpage
